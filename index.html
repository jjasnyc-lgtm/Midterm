Harlem Plaza Redesign

About the Project

This project redesigns Montefiore Square into a safer, greener, and community-focused urban plaza.

The Problem

The plaza lacks safety, seating, greenery, and meaningful public use, making it underutilized.

Site Context

Design Options

Green Plaza

Focus on nature and relaxation.

Market Plaza

Focus on activity and vendors.

Social Plaza

Focus on gathering and events.

Final Proposal

The final proposal transforms the plaza into a pedestrian-first space that prioritizes safety, green infrastructure, and flexible community use. New seating areas, improved lighting, and shaded zones encourage longer stays, while redesigned circulation paths reduce conflict between vehicles and pedestrians.

Reflection

AI helped generate design ideas and visuals, while human decisions guided the final concept and ensured it addressed real site conditions.
